Our Specializations & Services

Providing high-accuracy diagnostics, precision fabrication, and reliable repair and overhauling services to restore your hydraulic cylinders to optimal operational specs.

1. New Cylinder Manufacturing

Precision design and engineering of custom double-acting and single-acting hydraulic cylinders built to OEM specifications up to 350 bar.

2. Repair & Reconditioning

Complete overhaul of worn-out cylinders. Repair services include straightening, bore honing, re-tubing, and rod weld rebuilding.

3. Seal Kit Replacement

Replacement of degraded gaskets and seals using high-grade Polyurethane (PU), NBR, and PTFE seal systems for leak-free operations.

Learn More

4. Rod Re-chroming & Honing

Polishing and electro-plating worn piston rods with hard chrome finish to secure high corrosion resistance and low friction wear.

5. Pressure & Performance Check

Rigorous high-pressure static and dynamic test schedules to detect internal bypass and external leaks before client delivery.

Zero Compromise Standards

Quality Assurance & Key Benefits

Every hydraulic cylinder manufactured or serviced undergoes thorough stage-wise parameter evaluations to assure high durability and leak-proof performance under extreme conditions.

100% Pressure Tested Before Dispatch

Every cylinder is subjected to rigorous static and dynamic pressure tests on our custom hydraulic test stand to verify assembly integrity.

Strict Dimensional & Stage Checks

Strict stage-wise dimensional inspections on honed diameters, seal grooves, and concentricity guarantee micro-accuracy tolerances.

Long Life & Leak-Proof Performance

Integrated high-grade sealing materials (polyurethane, NBR, and carbon-filled PTFE seals) guard against wear and system fluid bypass.

High Load Capacity with Low Downtime

Heavy-duty construction using high tensile structural materials prevents deformation under peak load states and reduces maintenance cycles.

Advanced Coating

Technical Expertise
